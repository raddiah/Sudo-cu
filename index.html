Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.

What are your transfer limits?

Our standard transfer limits are $5,000 per day for external transfers and $10,000 per day for internal transfers. You can request higher limits by contacting customer support.

How do I set up Zelle?

Zelle is already enabled on your account! Just go to the Zelle tab, enter the recipient's email or phone number, the amount, and confirm the transfer. The first time you send to a new recipient, you may need to verify the details.

What should I do if my card is lost or stolen?

Immediately go to the "My Cards" section and click "Lock Card" to prevent unauthorized use. Then call our 24/7 support line at 1-800-SUDO-CU to report it. We'll cancel the card and send you a new one.
